## 1. Our Mission

**myKiboCFO** is the bridge between complex financial obligations and a peaceful life for Colombians. We are your personal financial ally.

### The Problem

Managing finances in Colombia is chaotic. Tax calendars are confusing, banks are fragmented, and "financial advice" is often disconnected from reality.

### The Solution: The "Kibo Loop"

1. **Ingest:** We aggregate scattered financial data (Banks + DIAN).
2. **Interpret:** We translate "transactions" into "behavioral patterns."
3. **Guide:** We align financial reality with personal dreams.

## 5. Product Principles

### Design Philosophy

- **Mobile-First:** Most Colombians access financial tools via smartphone.
- **Offline-Capable:** Internet connectivity can be unreliable; core features must work offline.
- **Minimalist UI:** Financial data is complex; the interface should be simple.

### Development Principles

- **Security > Speed:** Never compromise user data for faster deployment.
- **Local Context > Generic Features:** Colombian-specific features take priority.
- **Education > Automation:** Don't just automate; teach users why it matters.

## 6. Success Metrics

We measure success not just in downloads, but in **life impact**:

- **Penalty Avoidance Rate:** % of users who avoid DIAN penalties after using Kibo.
- **Dream Achievement Rate:** % of users who reach their defined financial goals.
- **Financial Peace Score:** User-reported stress reduction (measured quarterly).
