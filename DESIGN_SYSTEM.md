# Radical Clarity & Liquid Glass Design System
*Based on "The Outsiders" aesthetic.*

## 1. Design Philosophy
- **Concept**: High-performance cockpit. Sharp, precise, analytical.
- **The "Void"**: True Black backgrounds (#000000).
- **"Liquid Glass"**: Surfaces have depth, blur, and transparency.

## 2. Color Palette

### Backgrounds
- **Void Black**: `#000000` (Main Background)
- **Glass Surface**: `#1C1C1E` (with opacity & blur)
- **Card Surface**: `#2C2C2E`

### Functional Data Colors
- **Acid Green (Good/Future)**: `#CCFF00` or `#32D74B`
- **Electric Orange (Warning/Present)**: `#FF9F0A`
- **Cobalt Blue (Neutral/Living)**: `#0A84FF`
- **Critical Red**: `#FF453A`

### Typography
- **Primary**: `#FFFFFF`
- **Secondary**: `#8E8E93`

## 3. Components

### Liquid Cards
- **Shape**: Rounded corners (20px - 24px).
- **Style**: Dark grey fill + Low Opacity + Backdrop Blur (20px).
- **Border**: 1px White at 10% opacity.

### Typography Rules
- **H1 (Values)**: Bold/Heavy. Huge size.
- **H2 (Labels)**: Semibold, Uppercase, Tracking +1.5.
- **Data**: Monospaced font for numbers.

## 4. Usage
- Use **Bento Grid** layouts.
- Negative space is crucial.
